### The latest in EmSArcade meme based technology
#### *By VapidAntGames*


# Commands:

## !wynlyn
Tells you how long until @Lynenna#6247's 24h stream on May 21st 2071

ex:
`Lyn's 24h stream will start in: 18260 days 10:32:05`

## !wrightstonk

fetches the current market value of WRIGHT SOCKS

ex:
`YOU CAN GET A PAIR OF WRIGHT SOCKS TODAY FOR ONLY $14.00`

## !teatime

Tells you what tea to make next

(tea recipes are randomly generated from listings of actual tea recipes)

ex:

`I recommend you add  17% of Pai Mu Tan tea and 66% of shredded coconut and 17% of linden flowers and steep it for 28 minutes!`

## !hilda

Aunt Hilda gives you a piece of advice

(Hilda's advise comes from a dictionary I made of 100 pieces of advise from 100 year olds.
source: https://www.mentalfloss.com/article/54286/100-pieces-advice-100-year-olds)

ex:
`As Aunt Hilda always says... "I try to take the time to look at and appreciate the smaller things that make this life beautiful. When I do that, time slows."`
